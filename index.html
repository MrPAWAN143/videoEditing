What will you learn in Adv. Certification in Video Editing Course?

MODULE 1

Introduction to Video Editing

  • Content Outline : Basics of video editing, understanding the video editing workflow, introduction to video editing software.
  • Project : Edit a simple video using basic cuts and transitions.
MODULE 2

Fundamentals of Video Composition

  • Content Outline : Principles of video composition, storyboarding, sequencing, color grading basics.
  • Project : Create a storyboard for a short video project and edit a video incorporating these composition principles.
MODULE 3

Video Editing Techniques

  • Content Outline : Advanced editing techniques in Adobe Premiere Pro including keyframe animation, color correction, and working with multi-camera footage.
  • Project : Develop a complex video project that incorporates advanced editing techniques, color correction, and keyframe animations.
MODULE 4

Audio Editing for Videos

  • Content Outline : Basics of audio editing, syncing audio with video, sound effects, background scores, and voice-over integration.
  • Project : Edit the audio for a video project to enhance its narrative, including background music, sound effects, and voice-over.
MODULE 5

Motion Graphics & Composting

  • Content Outline : Introduction to After Effects, creating visual effects, motion graphics, and integrating them into video projects.
  • Project : Design and integrate motion graphics and visual effects into a video project.
MODULE 6

Real-world Applications and Portfolio Development

  • Content Outline : Building a portfolio, freelance video editing, working with clients, and insights into the video editing industry.
  • Project : Complete a capstone project that showcases the skills learned throughout the course, and prepare a professional portfolio.
MODULE 7

Internship and Career Preparation

  • Content Outline : Application of learned skills in real-world projects, internship with a production team, preparation for job placement in the video editing field.
  • Project : Engage in an internship to gain practical experience, culminating in a professional video project that can be added to your portfolio, followed by assistance with job placement.
